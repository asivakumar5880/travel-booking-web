Key Challenges

Problems We Needed
to Solve

The existing platform suffered from fundamental UX issues creating friction at every touchpoint.

01

Complex Navigation

Deeply nested menus and confusing architecture made it impossible for users to find what they needed.

02

High Bounce Rate

Landing pages failed to engage users, with overwhelming content and unclear value propositions.

03

Confusing Booking Flow

Multi-step booking was unintuitive, with too many fields and unclear progress indicators.

04

Low Mobile Usability

The mobile experience was a shrunken desktop view with tiny touch targets and scroll issues.

05

Poor Visual Hierarchy

Inconsistent typography, spacing, and color usage created a cluttered interface with no clear focus.

Objectives

Strategic Goals That
Drove Every Decision

Each design decision was anchored to measurable business goals and validated user needs.

5
Core Goals

Improve Conversion Rate by 40%

Streamline discovery to booking with reduced friction and clear CTAs.

Create a Clean, Modern UI

Build a visually appealing interface reflecting trust and quality.

Optimize Mobile-First Experience

Design for mobile users who make up 68% of platform traffic.

Simplify the Booking Process

Reduce checkout to 3 steps with smart defaults and auto-fill.

Enhance Visual Trust & Clarity

Social proof, clear pricing, and consistent design language.
